# Shipwrecked site issue map

## P0 — fix first
1. Legacy URL ecosystem is still live:
   - `/pages/*`
   - `/products/*`
   - `/blogs/news/*`
2. New blog article routes under `/blog/*` currently resolve to `Blog Post Not Found`.
3. Review/testimonial content is repeated heavily across homepage, services hub, and service pages.
4. Contact page still includes the live text: `This form is staging-safe.`

## P1 — next
5. Homepage H1 is brand-forward instead of keyword-forward.
6. Services hub H1 is copy-forward instead of keyword-forward.
7. Core service page H1s are short labels instead of explicit local-intent headings.
8. Location pages are too structurally similar and need differentiation.

## P2 — after that
9. Canonical, schema, sitemap, and robots rules should be audited after redirects are live.
10. Internal links should be tightened after titles/H1s and route targets are stable.
11. Analytics events should measure form submits and phone clicks on canonical routes.
12. CWV should be improved after route and rendering stability are in place.
