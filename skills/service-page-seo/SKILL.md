# Goal
Turn service pages into money pages that align with local search intent and convert leads.

# Required elements
- explicit service + location title tag
- explicit service + location H1
- plain-language intro
- what this service solves
- what is included / how it works
- why choose Shipwrecked
- FAQ section
- related service links
- related location links
- final CTA

# Guardrails
- Keep claims factual.
- Do not add fake pricing.
- Do not add FAQ schema just for rich-result hopes.
- Do not remove working CTAs.
