# Goal
Make each location page genuinely useful and distinct without creating doorway pages.

# Required approach
1. Compare the target location page against at least two sibling location pages.
2. Identify duplicate sections and rewrite only what needs differentiation.
3. Preserve route stability.
4. Emphasize service availability, route density language, FAQs, and links relevant to that location.
5. If unique local proof is missing, note the exact human inputs needed.

# Guardrails
- Do not invent neighborhood details, customer stories, or environmental claims.
- Do not make all locations identical with city-name swaps.
- Do not expand to new locations unless explicitly asked.
