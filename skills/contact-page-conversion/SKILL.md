# Goal
Turn contact pages into high-trust quote pages.

# Required checks
- remove staging or placeholder language
- clarify what happens after submission
- keep call/text alternative visible
- keep service-area language visible
- add trust proof or guarantee support if the layout allows
- make the primary CTA explicit

# Guardrails
- Do not change form field names or submission wiring unless that is the task.
- Do not remove the phone/text path.
