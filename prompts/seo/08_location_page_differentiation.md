# SEO 08 — Location page differentiation

**Suggested skill:** `location-page-localization`  
**Suggested agent:** `seo_copy_editor`

## Goal
Make the highest-priority location pages more distinct so they are useful support pages rather than city-name swaps.

## Scope
Start with South Abilene, North Abilene, and Abilene Wylie.

## Instructions to Codex

1. Compare `/locations/south-abilene`, `/locations/north-abilene`, and `/locations/abilene-wylie`.
2. Reduce duplicated framing and duplicated section wording.
3. Update title tags to remove the duplicated `| Shipwrecked Pools | Shipwrecked Pools` pattern.
4. Strengthen each page with:
   - unique intro framing
   - service emphasis that differs by page
   - route-availability wording that is not boilerplate
   - unique FAQ phrasing
   - clearer links to the best-matching service pages
5. If true local proof is missing, document the exact human inputs needed in `docs/human_inputs_needed.md`.


## Done when

- top location pages are less duplicative
- title tags are cleaned up
- service links are stronger and more contextual


## Constraints

- do not invent neighborhood claims
- do not create new location pages
