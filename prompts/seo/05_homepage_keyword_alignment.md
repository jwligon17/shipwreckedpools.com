## Goal
Make the homepage clearer for pool-cleaning search intent without losing the premium brand tone.

## Scope
Homepage metadata, hero copy, and any directly supporting CTA text only.

## Instructions to Codex

1. Read `docs/recommended_title_h1_meta.csv`.
2. Update the homepage title, H1, eyebrow/supporting copy, and meta description so the page leads with:
   - pool cleaning
   - weekly pool service
   - Abilene, TX
3. Keep the line `Protect Your Pool System. Get Your Saturdays Back. Keep Water Crystal Clear.` as supporting copy or eyebrow text, not the main H1.
4. Keep the existing phone/text/quote CTA structure.
5. If the homepage schema references the old H1/title intent, update it too.


## Done when

- homepage title is keyword-forward
- homepage H1 is keyword-forward
- brand line remains as support, not the primary heading
- CTA structure remains intact


## Constraints

- do not redesign the hero
- do not rewrite unrelated sections
